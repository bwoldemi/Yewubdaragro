About US

Yewbdar Sintayehu Agriculture Development Organization is a female-owned organization established in 2022. The organization obtained 205 hectares of land in Jawi Woreda, Awi Zone, Amhara Region, Ethiopia, for agricultural mechanization through investment. The company has participated in government wheat production initiatives and has successfully contributed to food security. The organization also acquired 10 hectares of irrigable land in the Rib River Irrigation Corridor located in Fogera District, South Gondar Zone, for the development of horticulture, seed multiplication, and the introduction of new technologies to support smallholder farmers.
